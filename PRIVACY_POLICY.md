# Privacy Policy

Bullsy respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Storage

All settings and usage data (such as timer settings and streak data) are stored locally on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Bullsy does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## User Controls

You can reset all app data from the settings screen at any time.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

## Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the project's GitHub repository.
